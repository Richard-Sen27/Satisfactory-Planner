Welcome to Satisfactory - Planner
"It's time to plan a factory!"

Here you can plan your most efficient production line with all details, like production rate, transmitions-speed (for the conveyor-belts), multilayer-factories, an import and export function, so you don't lose your hard brain-work. In future we also want to give you the possibility to convert your plan to a satisfactory blueprint!

Watch out pioniers!
How it works
It's actualy pretty easy.

You have your selection menu at the left. You can extend each category you want and click on the wanted building. On the right side are your options for the selected objecet. Here you can chagne options like rotation, color, Mk.-Type and so on. Be free to try and error.
The GUI should be as intuitiv as possible. If not, please send us a mail with your suggestion for improvements to example@mail.com.
